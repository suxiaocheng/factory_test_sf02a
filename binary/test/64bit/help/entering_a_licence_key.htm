Licence keys for BurnInTest Linux will be in the form;

 

-----START_OF_KEY-----

TestUser

#BIA3AGSBFARC4AR997AWNFC4FEAGWG42532EKDJ

CM3TRFAKUVDSQ8VAFJ9CYVFCYF9YDGESP8IS4JAE

W93VE49U337RABIN3APMDG2HV5XU3FQGE5YIY6AA

------END_OF_KEY------

 

You should paste the entire key, including the START_OF_KEY and END_OF_KEY flags into the key entry window on the registration screen.
